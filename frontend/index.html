        How it works
        
Signals in. Priced risk out. Humans on the gate.

      

      

        
01
Read the signals
NAV, attestations, repayment, collateral, activity — and off-chain sentiment the rulebook ignores.

        
02
AI re-underwrites
A hybrid PD scorecard + Z.AI memo reprices the IRS every epoch and emits an early-warning.

        
03
Autonomy gate
Reversible actions auto-execute; a default proposal halts for a 2-of-3 human quorum.

        
04
Compliant payout
ERC-3643 verification on every payout; senior/junior tranches absorb loss in order.
